The problem

Industrial software hasn't changed in twenty years. The plants have.

01

Alarm overload

Operators drown in thousands of alarms per day. Critical signals get lost in the noise. Standards exist to fix this — but adoption is manual and painful.

02

Manual rationalization

Alarm rationalization takes months of spreadsheet work. Every review cycle starts from scratch. The institutional knowledge lives in people's heads, not in systems.

03

No intelligence

Legacy DCS and SCADA systems record data but don't think. Pattern recognition, root cause analysis, predictive maintenance — all done manually, if at all.
