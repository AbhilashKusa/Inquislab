Marketing · Video

Remote · IndiaFull-timeWork with founder

You're the creative half of the marketing team. You take the problems, hooks, and stories the ground researcher brings you — and you turn them into short videos, posts, and campaigns that get watched.

Apply now → Ask a question
What you'll work on
  • Turn raw industrial stories into 30-90 second videos that explain the problem and the product
  • Use AI video tools (Runway, ElevenLabs, Descript, CapCut AI) to produce fast without a crew
  • Run the LinkedIn, Twitter, and YouTube accounts — post daily, reply daily
  • A/B test hooks, thumbnails, and first-3-seconds until something goes viral
  • Work with the product team to film real product demos that don't look like product demos
  • Turn customer wins into case-study videos that bring in more customers
What we look for
  • You've made videos people watched — YouTube, Reels, TikTok, anything with real views
  • You use AI creative tools and know which ones are actually good right now
  • A sense of what makes someone stop scrolling
  • You can ship rough-and-fast today and polish tomorrow — not the other way around
  • Bonus: any experience making B2B content, or selling something through video
Leadership & growth
  • You'll own the visual and video output of the company from day one
  • As we grow, you lead the creative side of marketing — hire, direct, build the content engine
  • You'll learn how to sell a technical product with video — one of the most valuable skills in modern GTM
